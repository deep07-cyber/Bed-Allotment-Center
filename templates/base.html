    About Us

    A Smarter Way to Manage Hospital Beds

    We allows hospitals to maintain accurate records of their bed capacities, including Normal Beds, ICU Beds, HICU Beds, and Ventilator Beds. Patients can register through the portal, check available facilities, and book slots as per their requirements. The system also prevents duplicate bookings by verifying patient details such as SRF ID, ensuring fairness and accuracy in allotment.

    • A smart way to find and book hospital beds without hassle. We connect patients with hospitals by showing real-time availability of Normal Beds, ICU Beds, HICU Beds, and Ventilator Beds. With just a few clicks, you can check hospital bed status, register your details, and book a slot instantly. Our goal is to make hospital admissions faster, easier, and more transparent – ensuring that you or your loved ones get the right care at the right time.

    Services

    To create a digital healthcare solution that bridges the gap between patients and hospitals, especially in times of emergency, by ensuring quick, reliable, and transparent bed allotment for all.

    Real-Time Bed Availability

    We provide up-to-date information on hospital bed availability, including Normal Beds, ICU Beds, HICU Beds, and Ventilator Beds, so patients can make quick and informed decisions.

    Easy Patient Registration

    Patients can register securely with their details (such as SRF ID, name, and contact information) and book a hospital bed with just a few clicks.

    Hospital Management System

    Hospitals can easily update their bed capacity in real time, ensuring that accurate information is available to all patients and authorities.

    Fair and Transparent Allotment

    Our system verifies patient details to avoid duplicate bookings and ensures that beds are allotted in a transparent and fair manner.

    User-Friendly Interface

    The platform is designed to be simple and intuitive, allowing both patients and hospital staff to use it without technical difficulty.

    Alerts and Notifications

    Patients receive instant updates about their booking status, ensuring clarity and reducing confusion during critical times.
